Reels Çarkı — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Reels Çarkı ("the App"), developed by Centaur Studios, collects, uses, and protects your information. Reels Çarkı is an entertainment spin wheel application available on Android.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 App Data

Reels Çarkı collects minimal data:

  • Spin history and energy state (stored locally on device)
  • User preferences and settings (stored locally on device)

No personal information is collected or transmitted to our servers.

1.2 Analytics & Crash Data

  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Crash reports for bug fixing

1.3 Advertising Data

  • Google AdMob: The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements
  • AdMob may collect device identifiers (Google Advertising ID) to serve ads
  • Rewarded video ads are shown to replenish energy

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide the spin wheel functionality
  • Display advertisements via Google AdMob
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Analyze anonymous usage patterns

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be shared with:

  • Google (AdMob, Firebase) — for analytics and advertising purposes

4. Permissions

Reels Çarkı requires the following permissions:

  • Internet Access: Required for loading advertisements and analytics
  • Network State: To check connectivity before loading ads

5. Data Retention

All app data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data. Analytics data is retained according to Google's retention policies.

6. Children's Privacy

Reels Çarkı is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Contact

For questions, contact us at: contact@centaurstudios.online
