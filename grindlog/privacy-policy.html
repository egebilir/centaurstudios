GrindLog — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how GrindLog ("the App"), developed by Centaur Studios, collects, uses, and protects your information. GrindLog is a fitness and workout tracking application available on iOS.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Workout & Fitness Data

GrindLog collects the following data to provide its core functionality:

  • Exercise logs, repetitions, sets, and weights
  • Workout history and personal records
  • Achievement and badge progress
  • User preferences and app settings

This data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage and is not transmitted to our servers.

1.2 Analytics & Crash Data

  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve the app
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Crash reports to identify and fix bugs

1.3 Advertising Data

  • Google AdMob: Advertising identifiers (IDFA) for serving ads in the free tier
  • PRO subscribers do not see ads, and ad-related data collection is minimized

1.4 Subscription Data

  • RevenueCat: Manages subscription status (GrindLog PRO)
  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple — we do not collect credit card or billing information

2. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On iOS 14.5 and later, GrindLog requests your permission before tracking your activity across other companies' apps and websites. You can change this setting at any time in your device's Settings → Privacy → Tracking.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide workout tracking and progress analytics
  • Process and manage PRO subscriptions
  • Display advertisements to free-tier users via AdMob
  • Improve app performance and fix crashes
  • Gamification features (badges, levels, streaks)

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be shared with:

  • Google (AdMob, Firebase) — for analytics and advertising
  • RevenueCat — for subscription management
  • Apple — for payment processing and App Store operations

5. Data Retention

Your workout data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all locally stored data. Analytics data is retained according to Google's and Apple's data retention policies.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the app
  • Opt out of personalized ads via iOS Settings
  • Deny tracking permission via ATT
  • Cancel your subscription via App Store Settings

7. Contact

For questions about GrindLog's privacy practices, contact us at: contact@centaurstudios.online
