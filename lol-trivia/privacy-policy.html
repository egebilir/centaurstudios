LoL Trivia — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how LoL Trivia ("the App"), developed by Centaur Studios, collects, uses, and protects your information. LoL Trivia is a League of Legends trivia game available on Android.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Game Data

  • Quiz scores, progress, and achievements (stored locally on device)
  • Game mode preferences and settings (stored locally on device)
  • Leaderboard rankings (stored locally on device)

No personal information is collected or transmitted to our servers.

1.2 Analytics & Crash Data

  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to understand game mode popularity
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Crash reports for bug fixes

1.3 Advertising Data

  • Google AdMob: The app displays advertisements including interstitial and banner ads
  • AdMob may collect device identifiers (Google Advertising ID) to serve relevant ads

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide trivia game functionality and track progress
  • Display advertisements via Google AdMob
  • Improve game content and performance
  • Analyze anonymous usage to enhance game modes

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob: Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase: Firebase Privacy

4. League of Legends Disclaimer

LoL Trivia is an unofficial fan-made application and is not endorsed by or affiliated with Riot Games, Inc. League of Legends and related trademarks are property of Riot Games, Inc.

5. Permissions

  • Internet Access: Required for loading advertisements and analytics
  • Network State: To check connectivity
  • Push Notifications: For optional daily reminders to play

6. Data Retention

All game data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

7. Children's Privacy

LoL Trivia is not directed at children under 13. The game contains no explicit content but is intended for League of Legends players aged 13 and above.

8. Contact

For questions, contact us at: support@centaurstudios.online
